EduLinker

EduLinker is a web application that has been developed to connect students of Sri Lanka with professional tutors. The platform enables students to readily identify the teachers in their subjects of concern for the Sri Lankan government school syllabus from grade 6 up to A/L. Due to the constant increase in the interest of the students towards online classes as well as seeking personal tutors, EduLinker provides one stop solution to the study needs of the students. The platform is designed in a way that guarantees effectiveness even when students have to learn online through the sessions or get a tutor to attend their residence for in-person instruction.
